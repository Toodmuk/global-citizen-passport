The journey

Four stages turn exposure into ability.

Each stage earns a stamp — and moves the needle on three skills no app can teach: adaptability, cultural awareness, and confident communication.

1 · SPARK
The Global Citizen Challenge. Take the free EF SET at your school, complete one real-world cultural task, and receive your physical Passport with its first stamp.
Skill unlocked: curiosity & self-assessment · KPI: EF SET starts
2 · IMMERSE
The real experience. A language camp, long stay, or academic year at EF’s own campuses — GCI scored before and after. This is the part that can’t be downloaded.
Skills built: all three, for real · KPI: weeks, GCI delta
3 · APPLY
‘Bring It Home.’ A four-week micro-project back in Thailand — run an English activity, host a cultural exchange — ending in a showcase. Evidence goes into the Passport.
Skill cemented: confident communication · KPI: completion
4 · BELONG
EF Global Citizens — for life. Mentor the next cohort, access scholarships and the global career network, meet at the annual Bangkok summit. The journey never ends.
Mindset: lifelong learning · KPI: engagement, referrals
