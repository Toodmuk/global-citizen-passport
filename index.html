The journey

Four stages turn exposure into ability.

Each stage earns a stamp — and moves the needle on skills no app can teach: adaptability, empathy, resilience, confidence, belonging.

1
SPARK
The Global Citizen Challenge. Take the free EF SET at your school, complete one real-world cultural task — and receive your physical Passport with its first stamp.
SKILL · curiosity & self-assessment  ·  KPI · EF SET starts
2
IMMERSE
The real experience. A language camp, long stay, or academic year at EF’s own campuses — GCI scored before and after. The part that can’t be downloaded.
SKILLS · all five, built for real  ·  KPI · weeks, GCI delta
3
APPLY
‘Bring It Home.’ A four-week micro-project back in Thailand — run an English activity, host an exchange — ending in a showcase. Evidence goes into the Passport.
SKILL · confident communication  ·  KPI · completion
4
BELONG
EF Global Citizens — for life. Mentor the next cohort, access scholarships and the career network, meet at the annual Bangkok summit. The journey never ends.
MINDSET · lifelong learning  ·  KPI · engagement, referrals
